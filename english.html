What is AnkiMine?

AnkiMine is a browser extension that enhances your language learning while you browse. When you select a word, it shows you definitions and allows you to add them directly to your study platform, such as Anki. Boost your learning process by enriching your online experience efficiently and effectively.

AnkiMine simplifies the way you find and learn new words on the web. When you select a word, it displays definitions and gives you the option to add them directly to your preferred study platform. Improve your fluency and vocabulary with this intuitive and efficient tool.
