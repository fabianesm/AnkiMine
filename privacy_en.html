Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Privacy Statement

AnkiMine does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable user data.

All information selected by the user (such as words or phrases) is stored exclusively on the local device using chrome.storage.local. This information is never shared with third parties or transmitted to any external server.

The extension does not access browsing history, passwords, geolocation, or any personal content. It also does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms.

The sole purpose of this extension is to enhance your language learning experience while fully respecting your privacy.

If this policy changes in the future, it will be posted on this same page.
